Why a Carbon Footprint Calculator?

A Carbon Footprint Calculator helps individuals or organizations estimate the amount of greenhouse gases, primarily carbon dioxide (CO2), emitted directly or indirectly through their daily activities or operations

What a Carbon Footprint Calculator Measures

  • Electricity Usage: Measures emissions from energy consumption based on regional energy mix and usage patterns.
  • Gas Usage: Calculates emissions from natural gas consumption, considering combustion-related CO2 emissions.
  • Transportation: Estimates emissions from personal or fleet vehicle usage, factoring in fuel consumption and vehicle efficiency.
  • Travel: Evaluates emissions from air travel based on distance flown and aircraft type.

Use of a Carbon Footprint Calculator

  • Personal Awareness: It raises awareness about individual or organizational carbon emissions, helping users understand their environmental impact.
  • Environmental Responsibility: Encourages responsible consumption habits by quantifying the carbon impact of everyday activities like electricity usage, transportation, and travel.
  • Goal Setting: Facilitates goal setting for carbon reduction strategies, aiding in personal or corporate sustainability efforts.
